## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Microphone use disclosed for Coach dictation
Store privacy materials SHALL disclose optional microphone / audio use for Coach chat dictation (user-initiated recording transcribed on the athlete’s Coach Watts instance; audio not retained as a chat attachment).

#### Scenario: Privacy checklist covers audio
- **WHEN** preparing App Store / Play data-safety answers for a build that includes Coach dictation
- **THEN** `docs/store-privacy-checklist.md` includes microphone/audio purpose copy suitable for those questionnaires
