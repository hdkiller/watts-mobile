Shipped in `feat/login-and-error-polish`: wordmark at `assets/images/wordmark.png`; redirect-URI/CLI block gated by `__DEV__ || isExpoGoRuntime()`. Release-mode device verify remains as OpenSpec task 2.3 / 3.2.
