# 025 — HealthKit / Health Connect prefill for check-in

**Area:** expansion · **Priority:** medium · **Effort:** L · **Status:** open

## Problem
Sleep hours and weight are typed by hand every morning even though the phone already knows them.

## Proposal
Read last night's sleep and latest weight from HealthKit / Health Connect and prefill the Log form (user confirms with one tap). On the roadmap's "later" list; compounds with issue 006. Mind the privacy checklist — no health metrics in analytics.
