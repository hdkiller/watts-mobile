# 058 — Today: Training Load / Monthly Progress / This Week sections overlap each other

**Area:** Today/layout · **Priority:** high · **Effort:** S–M · **Status:** done

## Problem
Reproduced live (and reported from device): in the lower Today stack the three stats sections render on top of each other:

- The Training Load & Form card's action links ("Sign out & sign in / Open web") draw **below the card's border**, outside it.
- The "MONTHLY PROGRESS" section label sits on that card's edge with almost no gap.
- The "THIS WEEK" heading draws **over** the Monthly Progress card's caption ("Month-to-date through day 20 · tap for chart & filters").

Accessibility frames confirm it's real layout, not a screenshot artifact: "THIS WEEK" starts at y≈0.785 while the Monthly card's frame extends to y≈0.802. The overlap **persists after scrolling away and back**, so it's not a transient entering-animation frame.

Code-wise the column is clean — [today/index.tsx](<../../app/(app)/(tabs)/today/index.tsx>) renders `TrainingLoadGlance` → `MonthlyProgressGlance` → `WeekGlanceStrip` as plain siblings with positive margins inside one `EnterSection` (`Animated.View` + `FadeInDown`).

## Supporting evidence (2026-07-20)
Metro logs showed Reanimated warning 10× that "Properties opacity, transform of AnimatedComponent(Pressable) may be overwritten by a layout animation" — see [062](./062.md), fixed alongside.

## Resolution (2026-07-20)
Root cause was **not** the entering animation: the glances swap a fixed-height skeleton `View` (`h-20`/`h-24`) for taller content at the same tree position, React reuses the native view, and the stale height style sticks — content overflows the frame and the next sibling is placed against the short height. Fixed by keying each branch (`key="skeleton"` / `key="content"` / `key="forbidden"`) in [TrainingLoadGlance.tsx](../../src/features/performance/TrainingLoadGlance.tsx) and [MonthlyProgressGlance.tsx](../../src/features/stats/MonthlyProgressGlance.tsx) to force a remount on swap; also removed the `FadeInDown` wrapper from the async stats section in today/index.tsx (defense in depth). Verified on device: cards contain their content, "This Week" sits clear of the Monthly card. Watch for the same skeleton-swap pattern in future glances.
