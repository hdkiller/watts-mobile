# 051 — Sports settings: title/subtitle duplication, raw enums, weak row affordance

**Area:** settings · **Priority:** low · **Effort:** S · **Status:** open

## Problem
Live on [settings/sports.tsx](<../../app/(app)/settings/sports.tsx>): each profile card prints the sport list twice — title "Swim, OpenWaterSwim" and directly under it the subtitle "Swim · OpenWaterSwim"; "WeightTraining" appears as both title and subtitle of its card. Combined with the raw camelCase enums (043) the screen reads like a debug view. The cards also lack a chevron or other affordance, so it isn't obvious they navigate to a per-sport threshold editor.

## Proposal
One humanized title per card (primary sport name, e.g. "Swim" / "Weight training"), the remaining variants as the subtitle only when they add information ("Also: open water"), thresholds line as-is, and a chevron on the right like the Settings hub rows. Depends on the `humanizeWorkoutType` helper from 043.
