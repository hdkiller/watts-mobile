# 050 — Athlete screen: scale-less AI score chips, dense wayfinding prose

**Area:** profile · **Priority:** low · **Effort:** S · **Status:** open

## Problem
Live on [athlete.tsx](<../../app/(app)/athlete.tsx>):

1. The AI profile card ends in bare-number chips — "Fitness 2 · Recovery 5 · Nutrition 6 · Consistency 1 · HR/Power 5". No scale (of 10?), no color, no direction; "Consistency 1" reads as either alarming or meaningless. Same problem as the activity analysis scores (048) on a different screen.
2. The "Edit metrics" intro is four lines of wayfinding prose ("For per-sport thresholds, use Settings → Sports. Distance, temperature, and timezone live under Settings → Units & locale. Zones and advanced Sport Settings stay on the web.") before the first field — a paragraph where a single line + links would do.

## Proposal
Give scores the shared treatment chosen in 048 (muted "/10" or mini bar/ring, low-score color cue) — build it once as a small `ScoreChip`/`ScoreRow` component used by both screens. Compress the Edit metrics intro to one line ("Default sport profile — per-sport and advanced settings live in [Sports] and on the web").
