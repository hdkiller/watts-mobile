# 059 — Scope drift shows a scary "sign out and sign in again" card on Today

**Area:** auth/Today · **Priority:** high · **Effort:** M · **Status:** done

## Problem
Live on Today: the Training Load & Form slot rendered a permanent error card — "**This session cannot read training load. Sign out and sign in again to refresh permissions.**" with a "Sign out & sign in" link ([TrainingLoadGlance.tsx](../../src/features/performance/TrainingLoadGlance.tsx); [AthleteProfileOverview.tsx](../../src/features/profile/AthleteProfileOverview.tsx) had a sibling copy).

Root cause: `COMPANION_SCOPES` grew after this session was authorized (the PMC/performance read), so the stored token 403s on the new endpoint. Every athlete who signed in before a scope addition hits this — a **recurring lifecycle event** — and the old treatment had three problems:

1. **Placement/tone**: a persistent permission-error card in the middle of the daily glance reads like something is broken, forever.
2. **The remedy was oversized**: "Sign out & sign in" called `signOut()` — nuking tokens, push registration, and query cache — when re-running `loginWithPkce` *without* signing out mints a token with the new scopes in one tap (the system-browser session is usually still valid).
3. **No general mechanism**: each feature screen hand-rolled its own 403 card.

## Resolution (2026-07-20)
Shipped the in-place update path: new shared [UpdateAccessCard.tsx](../../src/features/account/UpdateAccessCard.tsx) (friendly copy — "New permissions are available… **Update access**") re-runs `signIn()`/PKCE without signing out, then invalidates queries; wired into `TrainingLoadGlance` and `AthleteProfileOverview` (the athlete screen's `onReauth` now uses `signIn`, not `signOut`). Verified on device. **Deferred:** persisting the granted scope at token exchange + one proactive app-level drift prompt — per-feature 403 cards remain the detection mechanism for now.
